AUTONOMOUS DISCOVERY AGENT — THE "ANTIGRAVITY" BLUEPRINT (PC-ONLY)
============================================================
Goal: Transform the Media Tracker from a passive notebook into an active "Cinephile Intelligence Agency." This background agent scouts the internet, calculates "Gaps" in your knowledge, and presents them as high-priority suggestions.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
PHASE 0: THE AGENT'S BRAIN (DATABASE SCHEMA)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
We need three new local SQLite tables to support the Agent's memory:
1. `watched_history`: A local mirror of your Trakt/Letterboxd data (ID, Title, Year, LastSynced).
2. `found_gaps`: The Agent's inbox (Title, Year, DiscoverySource, GravityScore, StreamingLinks).
3. `eternal_blacklist`: Movies you've dismissed or aren't interested in (ID, Title).

─────────────────────────────────────────────
PHASE 1: THE MIRRORING LAYER (SYNC)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- FREQUENCY: Hourly pings via **APScheduler**.
- ACTION: Silently mirror your entire watch history into the local SQLite table.
- BENEFIT: Instant "Delta" calculations without hitting internet rate limits.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
PHASE 2: THE SILENT SCOUTS (DAEMONS)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
Four specialized background scripts that treat the internet as a "Target Map":

[A] THE CULTURAL BLINDSPOT DAEMON
- Scans TMDB for: "Top 50 movies with >10,000 votes and rating >8.0" for every year since 1950.
- Result: 5,000+ "Masterpieces" to compare against your history.

[B] THE DIRECTOR DEEP-DIVE CRAWLER
- Trigger: You rate a movie 9 or 10.
- Action: Scans that director's entire filmography (>7.0 rating) and flags missing titles.

[C] THE TEMPORAL AUDITOR
- Action: Identifies "Weak Years" in your movie timeline and fetches the top 20 hits from those eras.

[D] THE SISTER-FILM RECURSIVE SCANNER
- Action: Crawls 3 levels deep into "Similar Movies" for your Top 20 favorites.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
PHASE 3: THE DELTA & GRAVITY ENGINE
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- THE DIFF: `Internet Scans MINUS (Local Watched + Blacklist)`.
- GRAVITY SCORING: Suggestions are sorted by priority:
  * +10 pts: If directed by a "Liked Director" or on IMDb Top 250.
  * +5 pts: If available on your favorite streaming service (via JustWatch API).
  * +2 pts: High critical consensus.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
PHASE 4: THE "FOUND GAPS" INBOX (UI)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- PLACEMENT: Integrated as a "✨ Discovered Gaps" button inside the Admin "Add Entry" modal.
- ACTION:
  1. Click "Suggest" -> The Agent presents the top 10 highest "Gravity" gaps.
  2. One-click "Add to Watchlist" or "Log as Watched" (pre-fills all metadata).
  3. "Never Show Again" button to move a movie to the Eternal Blacklist.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
TECHNICAL STACK (THE "ANTIGRAVITY" STACK)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- ORCHESTRATOR: **Python (APScheduler)** — Handles all timing and background tasks.
- API WRAPPER: **tmdbv3api** — For clean, reliable movie metadata.
- AVAILABILITY: **JustWatch API** — To show where movies are currently streaming.
- MATCHING: **TheFuzz** — To handle "Dark Knight, The" vs "The Dark Knight."
- OPTIONAL: **OpenAI API** — To generate "Why you'd like this" reasoning for each gap.
