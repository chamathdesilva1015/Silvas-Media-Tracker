RETROACTIVE AUDITOR — FINAL IMPLEMENTATION PLAN
=========================================
Goal: Achieving a 1:1 map between your memory and your tracker. This system proactively finds movies you likely *already* watched throughout your life and allows you to permanently log them as manual entries.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
1. THE AUDIT PROCESS: "FIND → SUGGEST → LOG"
─────────────────────────────────────────────

[STEP 1: THE DISCOVERY]
The background script (Agent) crawls TMDB for "High-Probability" watches:
- CHILDHOOD PEAKS: Animation hits from your 3-13 age window.
- BLOCKBUSTER HITS: Top 50 most popular movies for every year of your life.
- FRANCHISE GAPS: If you have "Toy Story 1," it checks for "Toy Story 2 & 3."
- ACTOR/DIRECTOR STAPLES: Other films by actors/directors you've rated 8 or higher.

[STEP 2: THE SUGGESTION]
The Agent populates a local `audit_queue` table with these findings, filtering out anything already in your database.

[STEP 3: THE CONFIRMATION (UI)]
A dedicated "Audit Mode" page on your site presents these as cards:
- [✔] "WATCHED IT": Opens a mini-modal to select a quick score (default 7/10).
- [✖] "NEVER SEEN IT": Adds to the Eternal Blacklist so it never bothers you again.

[STEP 4: PERMANENT LOGGING]
Confirmed movies are added to the `MediaItem` table with:
- `source`: "manual" (or "retroactive_audit").
- `discord_id`: NULL (isolated from Discord sync).
- `date_added`: The current timestamp.
*Result: These entries are permanent, stay in your DB forever, and do not require any Discord activity.*

─────────────────────────────────────────────
2. THE ARCHITECTURE: "THE MEMORY MAP"
─────────────────────────────────────────────

[A] BACKEND: `audit_engine.py`
- A dedicated Python service that handles the TMDB API calls and the "Delta" logic.
- Uses `TheFuzz` for title matching to ensure we don't suggest something you already have.

[B] DATABASE: NEW TABLES
- `audit_queue`: Holds the temporary suggestions for your review.
- `eternal_blacklist`: Holds movies you've confirmed you *haven't* seen.

[C] FRONTEND: `audit.html` (PC-ONLY)
- A high-density grid optimized for rapid-fire confirmation.
- Includes a "Why am I seeing this?" tooltip (e.g., "Reason: Top 10 Popularity in 2008").

─────────────────────────────────────────────
3. WHY THIS IS WHAT YOU WANT
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- ZERO DISCORD CLUTTER: Your Discord channels stay clean. These go straight to the DB.
- AUTOMATED DISCOVERY: You don't have to remember movies; the Agent reminds YOU.
- DATA INTEGRITY: By filling these "Past Gaps," you make the "Future Discovery" agent 100% more accurate.
